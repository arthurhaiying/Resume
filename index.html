Hi！ I am a first-year PhD student in computer Science at UCLA. I am fortunate to work at the Automated Reasoning Group and be advised by Professor Adnan Darwiche.

My research interests focus on Probabilistic Modeling, Inference, and Learning. My goal is to combine symbolic approaches for expressing knowledge and uncertainties (e.g Bayesian Networks) with modern machine learning techniques to build AI systems that make decisions in a more transparent/reliable manner, as well as discover meaningful structures that underlie complex, real-world problems.

I also obtained my Bachelor degree in Computer Science at UCLA.
